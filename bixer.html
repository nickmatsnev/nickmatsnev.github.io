Let me tell you a great story about Bixer's creation!

It won't have happened if it is not Vladislav Sedov. We started this project as a school project thus we had many ideas what to do. At first we wanted to make an app, which helps to navigate drivers with nearest car washes and petrol stations, but then we realized how useless it would be, so we decided to move on with routine apps and got an offer from Dmitry and Konstantin Korischenko and Ougen Golod. Their idea included simple Bitcoin mixer, analytics of main chain and news about cryptoworld. Thanks to them we started our project.
